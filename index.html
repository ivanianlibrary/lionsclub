ABOUT US

Melvin Jones, a Chicago insurance agent,founded the first Lions Club in 1917 with the goal of creating a community service organization that would bring together local business people with a motto ‘We Serve’. Lions Club Adoor Kings was chartered through sponsorship by the Lions Club Adoor Aristo in the year 2023 with 30 members.
